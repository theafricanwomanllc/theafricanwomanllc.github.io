Our Services

Comprehensive Facilities & Custodial Solutions

Reliable and scalable service delivery for government agencies, school districts, and commercial operations. Every engagement is managed with precision and accountability.

01

Janitorial & Custodial

Daily and specialized cleaning services built for institutional and commercial environments.

Daily Cleaning & Maintenance

Comprehensive daily custodial services including surface cleaning, dusting, vacuuming, and common area maintenance to maintain pristine facility conditions.

Deep Cleaning

Thorough, scheduled deep cleaning services for high-traffic and specialized areas, ensuring long-term facility hygiene and presentation standards.

Restroom Sanitation

Complete restroom sanitization protocols including disinfection, fixture cleaning, supply replenishment, and compliance with health standards.

Floor Care — Buffing, Waxing & Stripping

Professional floor maintenance including strip-and-wax programs, high-speed buffing, and restorative treatments for hard floors across all facility types.

Trash Removal & Waste Management

Systematic waste collection, disposal, and recycling services aligned with facility management protocols and environmental compliance standards.

02

Facilities Support

Operational support services that keep buildings maintained, safe, and ready for use.

Building Maintenance Support

General building upkeep and maintenance coordination including light fixture replacement, minor repairs, and facility readiness assessments.

Event Cleanup

Post-event cleanup services for government buildings, convention centers, educational campuses, and commercial venues with rapid response and thorough execution.

Post-Construction Cleanup

Specialized cleaning following construction or renovation projects. We handle dust removal, debris clearing, surface polishing, and final inspection preparation.

Debris Removal

Safe, efficient removal of debris from construction, renovation, or storm damage while maintaining facility safety and operational continuity.
